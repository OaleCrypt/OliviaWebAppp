password_manager app
Objectives:
No Data Persistence: The app will not store any user data or passwords.
Client-Side Encryption: Demonstrate encryption purely on the client side.
No Display of Sensitive Data in Browser Developer Tools: Minimize exposure of user inputs in the console or inspection tools.
Showcase User Interaction: Provide a safe, interactive environment for users to see how encryption works.
